In the room

What a catalog can't do.

Most of what we specify for a house does not photograph honestly. A keypad's engraving needs a finger to trace it. A shade fabric needs to drape, not sit flat on a card. A speaker has a voicing that can only be heard in the air of a room, at the listening position it is being sold for.

The showroom is built around that fact. Everything on display is wired, powered, and ready to be used the way it would be used in your house. We put you in front of it, side by side with its alternatives, and let you decide.
