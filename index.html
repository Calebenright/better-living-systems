Our story

A family firm,
still on call.

Better Living Systems was founded in Winter Garden in 1979, back when "home automation" meant a thermostat you could program for the weekend. We've grown by staying small enough to know our customers and large enough to carry the licensing, insurance, and on-call staff a modern system demands.

We don't outsource monitoring. We don't subcontract installs. And we don't leave a job until the homeowner knows how to use the system.
