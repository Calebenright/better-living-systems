Our story · Est. MCMLXXIX

Three generations.
One phone number.

Better Living Systems has been a family firm in Central Florida since 1979, founded the same year Orlando added its first high-rise. We've watched the city grow. We've grown with it. And we've kept the same number since day one.

A brief chronicle

Forty-seven years of wires.

1979

Founded in Winter Garden

Started as a two-truck alarm company installing hardwired bell-box systems for citrus-country homeowners. No central station yet, just a phone tree.

1987

First commercial fire contract

Expanded into commercial fire protection with a Florida EF license and partnerships with local AHJs.

1994

24/7 central monitoring

Opened our own UL-listed monitoring operation. Still staffed by operators who know the properties.

2005

Smart home division

Added lighting and audio as home automation became wireless. First Lutron-authorized project that year.

2016

Third-generation leadership

Founder's grandchildren take operations. First dedicated home theater install the same year.

2026

Today

24 employees. Three divisions. 4,200 active residential accounts, 180 commercial. Still answering our own phones.

What we believe

Three things we don't outsource.

§ 01

The phone.

When you call, a human from this office picks up. Not a menu, not an offshore queue. If it's after hours, it's a technician, not a dispatcher.

§ 02

The install.

Every cable in your walls is pulled by a person on our payroll. We don't subcontract installs, and we don't rotate crews. The tech who finishes it is the tech you'll see again.

§ 03

The watch.

Our monitoring station is staffed by trained operators in our region. They know your floorplan, your pets, and the code word you'd use if something was wrong.
